BATTLEGROUNDS MOBILE INDIA

BGMI is India’s most popular battle royale game developed by KRAFTON. Fight, survive, and become the last player standing.

About BGMI

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is an online multiplayer battle royale game exclusively developed for Indian players by KRAFTON. It offers intense survival gameplay where up to 100 players compete on a large map to be the last one standing.

The game includes realistic graphics, tactical gameplay, multiple maps, ranked modes, seasonal rewards, and strong esports support in India.

Game Modes

Classic Mode – 100 players battle for survival
Arena Mode – Fast-paced close combat
Arcade Mode – Short and intense matches
EvoGround – Special themed modes
Ranked & Unranked Modes

Maps in BGMI

Erangel – Classic balanced map
Miramar – Desert sniper map
Sanhok – Small jungle map
Livik – Fast matches
Vikendi – Snow-themed map

Weapons & Equipment

BGMI includes a wide variety of weapons such as Assault Rifles, Sniper Rifles, SMGs, Shotguns, Pistols, Grenades, and Melee weapons. Attachments like scopes, grips, magazines, and muzzles enhance combat performance.

Vehicles

Vehicles help players move quickly across maps and include bikes, cars, jeeps, boats, and armored vehicles. Each vehicle has unique speed, durability, and terrain handling.

BGMI Esports

BGMI has one of the biggest esports ecosystems in India with official tournaments, prize pools worth crores, and professional teams competing at national and international levels.

System Requirements

iOS: iOS 11.0 or later
